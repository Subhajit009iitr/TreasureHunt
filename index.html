ACM

ACM IITR aims to bring together the computing community at IITR , allowing students to learn and share their knowledge, we are an open group of enthusiasts who foster general and scientific interest in computer science, our main events include open discussions, projects and workshops related to core computer science projects


Achievements

Multiple selections in open source programs like Google Summer Of Code, and The Linux Foundation, and also medals won at the Inter IIT Tech Meet We already have released multiple open projects , available on our social channels, we invite freshers to work on these , other than those we also invite contributions to our projects hosted on our github page, including KSOS and Symphonia

QCG

Quantum Computing Group (QCG) is an open group that aims to uplift and inculcate the culture of Quantum Computing at IIT Roorkee through various discussions, workshops, hackathons and open projects based on various topics in the vast and intriguing field of Quantum Computing.
We also participate in international hackathons and challenges based on Quantum Computing. Some of our core members have published research papers in the field of QC.

Achievements

A team from QCG was ranked 34 out of 700 in QHack 2023, a quantum computing hackathon.
A member of QCG participated in and secured 2nd place in iQuHACK (Microsoft-IonQ Challenge), MIT's annual quantum hackathon in 2022.

VLG

VLG is an open research group working in the field of machine learning and deep learning trying to foster an environment in the campus that helps peeps interested in doing applied and fundamental research in machine learning and adjacent fields


Achievements

VLG as a group submitted a research paper to the AAAI conference on artificial intelligence, our members also got their papers accepted at CVPR alongside some other of the most reputed research conferences in AI/ML, many of our group members participated in the Inter IIT meet and bagged top positions for the same
